Cancellation & Refund Policies

SMilE Andhra sincerely hopes that every registered participant will be able to attend the conference. However, we understand that unforeseen circumstances may arise. The SMile Andhra Cancellation and Refund Policies are as follows:

1) SMilE Andhra Summit Refund and Cancellation Policy:

1. Conference registration canceled before January 14, 2024, is refundable but subject to a non-refundable administrative fee of ₹990.
2.Registrants canceling on or after January 14, 2024, will NOT receive a refund
3.The non-refundable administrative fee will be deducted for cancellations made before January 14, 2024.
4.Registrants have the option to transfer their registration to another individual. In such cases, the invoice for the new registration will be revised to reflect the new registrant's confirmation status at a fee of ₹990.


Refund Process:

Refunds for eligible cancellations will be processed within 45 business days after receiving the cancellation request


Unforeseen Circumstances Defined for Refund and Cancellation Policies:

Unforeseen circumstances refer to unexpected events that hinder SMilE Andhra from continuing with a conference, training, or webinar. Examples include but are not limited to inclement weather, natural disasters, site unavailability, technology challenges, and presenter absence.


Contact Information

For questions or to cancel registration, please contact our support team at ed@smileandhra.in.


Important Notes

Registrants are encouraged to carefully review the cancellation deadline to ensure compliance with the policy.
Any adjustments to the new registrant's membership status will be communicated and managed accordingly
