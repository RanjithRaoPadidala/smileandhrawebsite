SMILE Andhra – the Industrial Innovation and Investment Summit of Progressive Andhra Pradesh, a dynamic event that unites visionaries, startups, investors, and industry leaders. This is your gateway to a world of possibilities, where groundbreaking ideas find their platform and where Andhra Pradesh’s growth story continues to unfold.

We foster innovation, empower startups, and drive economic growth across Andhra Pradesh. We envision a future where innovation knows no bounds, where entrepreneurs dream big, and where the spirit of progress is unwavering. By connecting talented minds with seasoned experts, we’re paving the way for a brighter, more innovative state.
